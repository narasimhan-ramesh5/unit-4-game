Crystal Collector!

How to play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This will be your target score.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

The value of the crystal is hidden. When you click on it, you will realize its value because it gets added to your total score.

The goal is to reach the target score. If you reach the exact target score, you win. If you go over, you lose.

May the crystal of fortune be with you!
